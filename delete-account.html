StageRoot — Delete Account & Data

Last updated: March 2026

How to Delete Your Account

You can delete your account and all associated data directly in the app:

1. Open StageRoot

2. Go to Profile tab

3. Tap Settings icon (top right)

4. Scroll to "Danger Zone"

5. Tap "Delete Account"

6. Confirm deletion

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, the following data is permanently removed:

- Your profile information (name, username, email)

- All video recordings and audio files

- All AI feedback and analysis results

- Practice history and progress data

- Streak and benchmark records

- Credit balance and transaction history

- Subscription information

Data Retention

- Video and audio files are deleted from servers within 24 hours of analysis, regardless of account status.

- Upon account deletion, all remaining data is permanently removed immediately.

- No data is retained after account deletion.

Alternative: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app, email us at support@stageroot.app with the subject "Delete My Account" and we will process your request within 48 hours.
